Lasagna

Lasagna with bechamel and tomata sauce. This recipe includes an original twist! Not only is it made with tomato sauce but it brings the warm fall flavour by including pumpkin.
Ingredients
  • 1 union
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 250 grams of mushrooms
  • half a pumpkin
  • 100 gram red lentils
  • 800 gram tomato
  • vegetable stock
  • 75 gram wheat flower
Steps
  1. Bake the union and garlic in a bit of olive oil.
  2. Add the mushrooms
  3. Add the pumpking and the lentils
